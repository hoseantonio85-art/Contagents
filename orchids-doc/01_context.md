# Product Context

This is an AI-powered operational risk management system.

Main goal: Help risk managers understand:

- what is happening
- why it is happening
- what to do next

This is NOT a dashboard. This is a decision-making interface.

Core entities:

- Corporate risks (master risks)
- Risk manifestations in objects (products, agents, counterparties)
- Incidents
- Measures

Key principle: The interface must explain and guide, not just display data.
